Frequently Asked Questions

How does CompanionBot work?

CompanionBot integrates with Telegram to provide AI conversations. Create a persona with custom instructions, and your AI companion starts learning from your conversations. It uses your chosen language model (Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini) and maintains context across all messages.

Which AI models can I use?

We support Claude 3, GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Google Gemini, and more. Pro and Enterprise tiers can switch between models instantly. Your conversations can use different models depending on what works best for each task.

Can I customize my AI's personality?

Absolutely! Write custom system prompts, define personality traits, set conversation rules, and control response styles. You have complete control over how your AI behaves and communicates.

Is my data private?

Yes. We encrypt all conversations end-to-end. Your personal data and conversation history stay private. We don't use your data to train models or share it with third parties. Enterprise customers can host on private infrastructure.
